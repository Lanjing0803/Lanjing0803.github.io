Fox Garden

April 19, 2021

Fox garden is an imaginary world on my sketchbook about one fox in a garden, illustrating perception of time, space, and movement. Using non-linear narrative, I want to depict one-day activities of the fox in the garden. I think non-linear narrative can help with the randomness here.
